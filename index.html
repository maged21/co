About Us

Aziz Co is a fashion consulting agency based in Dubai, UAE & Cairo, Egypt that was established in 2022. Our focus is to help fashion brands rethink strategy and optimize operations to meet the changing demands of consumers, production, and supply networks.

Services

Art Direction

The creative vision and guidance of a fashion shoot or campaign. It involves the coordination of all aspects of a fashion shoot, from concept development to styling, photography, and post-production.

Branding

the process of creating a unique identity for your brand. It involves the use of visuals, messaging, and other elements to create an image that resonates with customers and stands out from competitors.

Business development

the process of creating and implementing strategies to grow a fashion business. It involves researching the market, identifying opportunities, developing marketing plans, and launching products or services.

Fashion designing

Fashion design is the art of creating clothing and accessories that are aesthetically pleasing and functional. It involves the use of fabrics, colors, textures, and silhouettes to create unique designs that reflect current trends in society.

Influencer marketing

Influencer marketing is a form of marketing that focuses on using key leaders to drive your brand’s message to the larger market.

Photoshoots

Our photoshoots service includes professional styling, art direction, makeup, hair, high-end photography and photo retouching.

Social media

Social media management is the process of creating, scheduling, analyzing, and engaging with content posted on social media networks in order to achieve your marketing and branding goals.

Web designing

It encompasses several different aspects, including webpage layout, content production, and graphic design. Our expertise of web designers use a variety of tools to create a website that meets the needs of the client and their target audience.
